Player Support Center

Need Help?

Thank you for playing Horizon Skies. Our goal is to provide an enjoyable experience for every pilot. If you experience technical issues, account problems, purchase questions, or would like to report a bug, we're here to help.

Contact Support

Email:

bluhorizongamedevelopment@gmail.com

We typically respond within 24–72 business hours.

When Contacting Us

To help us resolve your issue as quickly as possible, please include:

  • Your device model
  • Your Android version
  • Your Horizon Skies game version
  • A detailed description of the issue
  • Screenshots or screen recordings (if available)
  • The date and approximate time the issue occurred

Frequently Asked Questions

I lost my game progress.

If Cloud Save was enabled, we will do our best to help restore your progress. Please contact support with your account information.

I made an in-app purchase but didn't receive my item.

Please include your Google Play purchase receipt or Order ID so we can investigate.

The game crashes or freezes.

Restart your device, ensure you're running the latest version of the game, and contact us if the issue continues.

I found a bug.

We appreciate bug reports. Please describe exactly how the issue occurred and include screenshots whenever possible.

Feedback

We love hearing from our players. If you have ideas for new aircraft, missions, airports, weather effects, or gameplay improvements, feel free to send us your suggestions. Community feedback helps shape future updates for Horizon Skies.
